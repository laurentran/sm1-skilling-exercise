Joint discovery workshop between Crestmont Financial and Meridian Advisory Group to align on the scope, objectives, and constraints for Crestmont's core-banking modernization program, ahead of Meridian's proposed approach.

Agenda

- Introductions and goals for the engagement
- Current core-banking landscape: mainframe constraints, release cadence, and COBOL dependency
- Data and reporting pain points: fragmentation across 14+ systems and manual regulatory reporting
- Regulatory drivers: recent OCC examination findings and remediation priorities
- Modernization objectives and guardrails: phased approach, no big-bang cutover, minimal disruption
- Success criteria, timeline, and budget expectations
- Next steps and proposal timeline (Meridian to submit the proposed approach by Friday)

Attendees: Janet Ross (SVP Technology & Operations, Crestmont Financial); Dana Cole (Managing Director, Meridian); Amara Diallo (Data & AI Practice Lead, Meridian).
